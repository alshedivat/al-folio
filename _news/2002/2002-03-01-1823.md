## MacIntosh AGAVE / GeoWall


Link: [http://www.evl.uic.edu/aej/macagave](http://www.evl.uic.edu/aej/macagave)

Mac AGAVE Set-up at EVL

![image](https://www.evl.uic.edu/output/originals/macagave.jpg-srcw.jpg){:style="max-width: 100%"}
Credit: A. Johnson, EVL

The MacIntosh AGAVE / GeoWall consists of a dual 1Ghz PowerPC G4 with 512M RAM and a GeForce 4MX graphics card connected to two InFocus 530 projectors mounted on a Chief Slide Projector rack that sits on a portable cart from Anthro. This configuration yields a portable <a href='http://www.evl.uic.edu/cavern/agave/'>AGAVE</a> system that can easily be move around the elementary school where vitual reality applications are being used in the educational curriculum. Linear passive stereo is front-projected onto a Da-Lite portable screen for a total system cost of less than $13,000.
