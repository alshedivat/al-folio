EVL faculty member and professor of Design Daria Tsoupikova will represent UIC at the 6th Annual International CAAV Conference to be held as a hybrid event at Purdue University on November 1 - 4, 2021.  Tspoupikova will participate in a panel discussion on Diversity, Equity, Inclusion, (DEI) and Accessibility, scheduled Wednesday, November 3rd at 1:30 pm EST.<br><br>
Tsoupikova is currently involved in several DEI activities, in particular a long-term project with Chicago&rsquo;s Goodman Theater on a virtual-reality-based theater experience to inform some of Chicago&rsquo;s youth who live in disadvantaged neighborhoods on the value of theater by making it experiential and relevant.  She will discuss her projects and experiences in affecting DEI.
