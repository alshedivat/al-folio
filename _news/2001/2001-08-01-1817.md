## Remote Data Mining, Visualization and Collaboration on Tera-Nodes


Link: [http://www.evl.uic.edu/cavern/teranode](http://www.evl.uic.edu/cavern/teranode)


![image](/images/tera.jpg-srcw.jpg){:style="max-width: 100%"}

The goal of this project is to develop advanced data mining, visualization and collaboration technologies that maximize the  use of high-speed Gigabit and Terabit international optical networks such as StarLight&#8480; to help researchers accelerate scientific discovery.
