## man+machine


man+machine

![image](/images/man+machine.gif){:style="max-width: 100%"}
Credit: Todd Margolis

Man+Machine is one in a series of ImmersaGrams that portray a clinical analysis of the evolution of the human species when aided by machine technology.<br><br>

The 3D images have a similar aesthetic to X-Rays, but with an added third dimension. The stark contrast of bone, muscle and tissue against wire, microchips and gears present the viewer with an unnatural juxtaposition. The subtle movement of the gears and muscles adds vitality to an otherwise cold and lifeless image.
