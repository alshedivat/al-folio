## Liquidon


Link: [http://www.liquidon.org](http://www.liquidon.org)

liquidon218

![image](/images/liquidon.jpg-srcw.jpg){:style="max-width: 100%"}
Credit: J. Tremonti / EVL

Liquidon is an immersive, realtime, audio-visual composition that explores the field of ITC.<br><br>

ITC, an acronym for Instrumental Trans-Communication, is a field of research pertaining to the use of technological apparatus to facilitate communication with the dead.<br><br>

Liquidon incorporates stereoscopic projection and an interactive, multi-channel soundscape.
