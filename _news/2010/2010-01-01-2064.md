## Predictive Computational Models for Orthopaedics

![image](/images/predictivecomputation.gif){:style="max-width: 100%"}
Credit: U Pitt

We developed a framework for integrating measured data - such as medical images, tracked motion, and anatomy-book knowledge - into the physically-based simulation of anatomical joints. The resulting models and simulations can be used to analyze medical data collected from a specific individual.
