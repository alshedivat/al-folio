## MALO Drama


Link: [http://www.evl.uic.edu/samt/malo_drama](http://www.evl.uic.edu/samt/malo_drama)


![image](/images/malo.gif){:style="max-width: 100%"}
Credit: S. Thongrong, EVL

MALO Drama is an interactive, immersive CAVE&trade; art application that allows users to communicate with a virtual wizard, a Magic Living Object (MALO), and other networked participants.<br><br>

The virtual wizard accompanies the user through the multi-dimensional environment. MALO Drama is a public exhibition of MFA thesis work by Samroeng Thongrong.
