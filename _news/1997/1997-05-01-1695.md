## Computer Animation Using Digital Video


Link: [http://www.evl.uic.edu/costigan/S97](http://www.evl.uic.edu/costigan/S97)


![image](https://www.evl.uic.edu/output/originals/jcostigan_networkedvideo.png-srcw.jpg){:style="max-width: 100%"}
Credit: J. Costigan, EVL

&ldquo;Computer Animation Using Digital Video&rdquo; is a web-based presentation providing a summary of existing video formats, animation systems and networked video protocols. Originally presented at SIGGRAPH 1997, the <a href='http://www.evl.uic.edu/costigan/S97/'>slide presentation</a> is a good resource for basic technical information compiled on digital video.
