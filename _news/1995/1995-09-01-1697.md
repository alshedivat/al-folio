## Aquasphere


Link: [http://www.evl.uic.edu/acruz/PROJECTS/aqua.html](http://www.evl.uic.edu/acruz/PROJECTS/aqua.html)

A Feeding Frenzy

![image](/images/aqua3.gif){:style="max-width: 100%"}
Credit: Alan Cruz

This application was an experiment in trying to create a very realistic environment by using complex models other than texture maps. The application was part of a VR compilation for SIGGRAPH 96 and Ars Electronica.
