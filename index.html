Kim Lab

Physics of Life -- From Molecules to Living Cells

Many body problems in biology at the single-molecule level

We are a new, interdisciplinary lab in the Department of Physics at UIUC. We are interested in physical principles underlying molecular interactions and functions inside the cell. Currently, we study spatio-temporal dynamics of RNA polymerases during transcription and RNA degradosome in Escherichia coli.

We are hiring! We are looking for highly energetic and motivated graduate students and postdocs with backgrounds in physics, chemistry, and biology. Please contact Sangjin via email.
